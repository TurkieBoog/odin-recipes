How to create a human via Alchemy

old human giving a thumbs up from the comfort of his chair

This recipe is a fine blend of many earthly elements mixed with a splash of early 1900s heavy grade alchemy found only in the Full Metal Alchemist Cinematic Universe put together to create a human. Humans are creatures found in fascinating spots such as "sidewalks", "grocery stores", and "houses". They have many fascinating uses though the main ones being industrialization, and existential crises. Lastly, and most importantly, they are NOT edible. Side effects include lost limbs, people in the vicinity disappearing, and possible death (at least according to the anime)

do not try this recipe under any circumstances, highly unadvisable

Ingredients:

  • 35l water
  • 20kg carbon
  • 4l ammonia
  • 1.5kg lime
  • 800g phosphorus
  • 250g salt
  • 100g potassium nitrate
  • 80g sulfur
  • 7.5g fluorine
  • 5g iron
  • 3g silicon
  • trace amounts of ~15 other elements
  • a human soul (this part is quite important)

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients together into pan or bowl
  2. Create chalk sigil on floor
  3. place pan/bowl in centre of sigil
  4. Prick your finger for a dash of blood
  5. Slap hands on sigil
  6. (optional) run away very fast
